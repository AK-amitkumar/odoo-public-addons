Russian Addresses Auto Complete

KLADR/FIAS addresses on a partner form

The app provides Odoo sync with the cloud service kladr-api.ru. Its goal is to automatically check and complete addresses in Russia

Features

  • Automatic complete of a region and a district by entering a city
  • Automatic complete of a zip code by entering city, street and house
  • Hints while entering region, city, district, street, house
  • The app doesn't forbid to enter any data even not stated in KLADR
  • Region is not a classifier but a text field
